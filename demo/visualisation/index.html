House prices and affordability in England and Wales

Introduction

House price is a great concern for UK people for a long time. BBC news reported that house prices are still increasing in UK reigions even if the speed of growth is slowing down except the south east of the UK where prices are rising fastest [1]. Median house price, as the market value of property in that area, can aviod to be skewed due to outliers, which is the reason why average house price is not adopted. For example, a property might be sold for a price far higher or lower than typical for the area [2]. Therefore, a research need to be investigated that the trend of median house price in the England and Wales and to explore whether local people can afford local house prices and find the related influence factors to predict the future tendency of house price and people's affordability.

  • Also, the location distribution, as a geographical factor, influence property price. Firstly, the regions in England and wales can be divided into 2 parts, one is named the south part including East of England, London, South East and South West parts, and the remaining regions can be named north part. Thus, there is a significant difference in median house price between south part and north part in England and Wales, which can be seen clearly from the map that properties in south part and have higher house prices than north part.
  • Secondly, the difference in property price between London and outlondon is significant. London has the most highest house price than other regions in England and Wales every year.

      • Summary

      Overall, house price is increasing in England and Wales over the past two decades, thus making more and more people can not afford their house espically for people who live in the south part of regions like london. Some factors like geography and unemployment rate to influence house price have indicated that there will still be a increasing in house price in England and Wales in the future, which means people who live in south part will still can not affordable, but north part of people have little influence.
